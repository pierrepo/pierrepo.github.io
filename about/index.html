Hi there!

My name is Pierre Poulain. I am a lecturer / (tenured) associate professor at Université de Paris.

My research is hosted at Institut Jacques Monod. In tight collaboration with wet-lab biologists, I develop new methods and software, mostly in Python, to analyse mass spectrometry data. More generally, I have a strong interest in data analysis and data management in biology.
